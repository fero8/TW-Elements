Introduction

Icons play a crucial role in enhancing user interfaces and creating visually appealing designs. We have integrated most popular icon libraries into our components and made it easy to customize them. Let's take a look at how to use them.

Top-layer customization

It is required to place icons inside span tags to provide better and easier customization. You can use any icon library you want, but we recommend using Heroicons as they are the most popular Tailwind ones.

Important: Take note of our approach to styling icons. We exclusively apply Tailwind classes to span tags, maintaining the icons in their original state. These are our primary guidelines for customization.
